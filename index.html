Our History

We make and grow the best coffee in Peru,
accompanying families since 1930, with professional
workers who harvest, collect and select the coffee with
quality work, thus providing exquisite coffee to enjoy
together as a family.

Steps of manufacturing our products

Harvest occurs annually when the
coffee beans reach maturity and
are collected for processing.

The beans are dried using a wet
or dry technique, depending on
the taste we want to obtain.

The coffee is roasted
and acquires its flavor by processing
the grain in ovens.
